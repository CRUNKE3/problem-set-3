Preparation

There are many different ways and styles to prepare a meal. While some ways to prepare a meal include cooking or spreading some peanut butter
and jelly, there are much more complex ways that require more steps and a lot more effort.


Cooking

Cooking is one of the most common ways to prepare a meal. You just get the necessary food items, mix them together, and put them in a pot or pan
to heat them up to a point of making the flavors blend and taste good.

Cooking also has more complex ways of being done. These styles include:

  • Thai
  • French
  • Indian
  • And Many More

All of these different cuisines have the same goal to produce some food that is tastey and good to eat. They also all use spices to bring out flavors in
famous meals.
